Word Count Guide

This guide explains recommended word counts for essays, blog posts, SEO articles, and professional writing.

Essay Word Counts

College essays typically range from 500–2000 words depending on assignment type.

SEO Word Counts

SEO articles perform best between 1,000–2,500 words depending on competition.

Blog Posts

High-performing blog posts average 1,500+ words.

Why Word Count Matters

Word count affects readability, ranking, engagement, and conversions.
